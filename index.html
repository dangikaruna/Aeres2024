About AERES 2024

The Aerospace Research Symposium (AERES) serves as an invaluable platform for the bright minds in Aerospace Engineering at the prestigious Indian Institute of Science. It offers students the opportunity to showcase their groundbreaking research in the fields of Aeronautics and Space technologies. After a brief pause, we are excited to announce the return of the Aerospace Research Students' Symposium for the year 2024.

Once in Lifetime Experience

AERES 2024 invites students to share their cutting-edge research through diverse presentation formats, including oral, poster, and video presentations. In addition to these student-focused sessions, AERES 2024 will facilitate the convergence of academia and industry through engaging invited talks and interactive workshops. This symposium offers a unique platform for various industries to exhibit their portfolios and connect with students, fostering valuable interaction through enlightening keynote speeches.

Enhanced Learning

We cordially invite you all to partake in this 4-days extravaganza. Thank you !

Poster Presentation

Research posters summarize information or research concisely and attractively to help publicize it and generate discussion. The poster is usually a mixture of brief text and tables, graphs, pictures, and other presentation formats.

Workshop: Ansys-FSI

Workshop on Ansys Fluent Fluid Structure Interaction with Ansys Mechanical. The training will present Hyper-elastic flap System coupling with solver settings and troubleshooting.

Video Presentation

Nothing communicates better than a small video of your experiments or simulations. All students are encouraged to take informative videos of their research and send them to us for presentation.
