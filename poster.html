Poster Presentation

Research posters succinctly summarize information of research in an attractive manner to publicize and spark the discussion. Typically, they incorporate concise text alongside tables, graphs, pictures, and various presentation formats.Poster presentations are cumpolsory for Ph.D. scholars in their 3rd year who have passed their comprehensive exam. MTech(R) students in their 2nd year must choose one out of either oral or poster presentations. The best two posters in the department will be awarded with exciting prizes! â€‹

Presentation Requirements

The poster must include:

  • Motivation for the work.
  • Introduction to your topic.
  • Objective and methodology followed.
  • Results and discussion.
  • Future work.
Submission Requirements

Poster size should be A1 (59.4 cm x 84.1 cm). (Poster Template)

Submission Guidelines
  • The deadline for the abstract submission of Poster presentation is December 10, 2023. All abstracts must be mailed to aerosymposium@iisc.ac.in in PDF format.
  • All abstracts must be written in LaTeX. Use the provided template file to write the abstract. (Download Template)
  • Use subject line- 'Abstract for Poster presentation AERES2024 - $Name_SRNo$'. The file name of the abstract should be Abstract_Poster_$Name_SRNo$.pdf.
  • The deadline for the presentation submission is December 23, 2024. The presentations must be mailed to aerosymposium@iisc.ac.in in PDF format. Use subject line - 'Poster presentation AERES2023 - $Name_SRNo$'. The file name of the presentation should be Presentation_$Name_SRNo$.pdf.
